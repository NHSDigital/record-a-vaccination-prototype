Log in with NHS CIS2

  1. Press the share button

    The share button looks like this.

    It is on the toolbar at the top or bottom of your screen.


  2. Select the NHS CIS2 app

    Look for this icon in the list of options


  3. Follow on-screen instructions

    You will be asked to use Touch ID or Face ID.

Troubleshooting

If you can't see the app, check you have the NHS CIS2 app installed.

If you continue to encounter problems, contact your RA.
