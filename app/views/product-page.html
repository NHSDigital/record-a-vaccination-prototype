How it works

1 Sign up

Sign up with your NHS email and get an account.

2 Get ready

Set up accounts for your colleagues and add vaccine products.

3 Record vaccinations

Check a patient’s history and record vaccinations you give.

These will automatically appear in the patient's GP record and in the NHS App.

4 Track performance

See how many vaccines you have given. Download data you need for reporting.

5 Get paid

Pharmacies are paid automatically by the NHS Business Services Authority through Manage Your Service (MYS).

You can switch point of care systems at any time, and use different systems for different pharmacies.

You will still have access to your previous system for at least 6 months so you can download reports.
