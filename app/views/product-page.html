How it works

1 Sign up

Sign up with your NHS email and get an account straight away.

2 Get ready

Set up accounts for your colleagues and add vaccine products.

3 Record vaccinations

Look up patients, check their vaccination history and record the vaccines given.

4 Track performance

See how many vaccines you are giving. Download data you need for reporting.

5 Get paid

Pharmacies are paid automatically by the NHS Business Services Authority (BSA).
