Your NHS region has invited you to start using the new NHS Record a vaccination service at {{ organisationName }}.

Here's what you need to do next:

1. Activate your Okta account

We've created an Okta account for you to securely access the service.

You’ll receive a ‘Welcome to Okta’ email (from noreply@okta.com). You need to activate the link within 7 days.

If you cannot find the email, check your spam or junk.

2. Log in to the Record a vaccination service

Once you’ve activated your Okta account, log in to www.ravs.england.nhs.uk using your Okta username and password.

You can also access the service through your Okta account by clicking ‘RAVS (PROD) app’.

3. Get ready to record vaccinations

Once you've logged in:

  • go to the ‘Vaccines’ section and add vaccines for your organisation
  • go to ‘Manage users’ to add more users to your organisation
  • if you are a clinician, go to your profile in ‘Manage users’ and select that you are a clinician
