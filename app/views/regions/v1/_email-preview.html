Your NHS region has invited you to start using the new NHS Record a vaccination service (RAVS).

The service allows you to safely and quickly record vaccinations. For example, COVID-19, Flu, RSV and Pertussis.

Get started

We've created an Okta account for you to securely access RAVS.

Here’s what you need to do:

Activate your Okta account

You’ll receive a ‘Welcome to Okta’ email (from noreply@okta.com). Please activate the link within 7 days.

If you cannot find it, please check your spam or junk.

Log in to RAVS

Once you’ve activated your Okta account, log in to www.ravs.england.nhs.uk using your Okta username and password.

You can also access RAVS through your Okta account by clicking ‘RAVS (PROD) app’.

Give yourself time to add your users and vaccines before your next vaccination campaign.

Many thanks, NHS Record a vaccination
