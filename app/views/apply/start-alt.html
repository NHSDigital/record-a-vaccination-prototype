Any community pharmacy offering NHS vaccinations can sign up.

What it involves

  1. You'll have the option of signing up for a single pharmacy or a for a group of pharmacies.
  2. Next, we'll ask you to select the pharmacy or pharmacies.
  3. Then you can nominate up to 5 lead administrators, including yourself, who will get access to RAVS at the selected pharmacy or pharmacies.
  4. Your nominated users will get an email with details of how to log in to RAVS.
  5. Once they have logged in, they will be able to add further users, including more lead administrators, at individual pharmacies.
  6. Anyone with lead administrator permissions can then add further users to the pharmacies where they have access to RAVS.

Signing up for a chain of pharmacies

If you have a large chain, you may want to consider signing up for a few pharmacies at the time.

This would allow you to have different lead administrators for each subset of pharmacies.

Before you start

If you have any questions about signing up, join a drop-in session with the RAVS team who will be happy to help
