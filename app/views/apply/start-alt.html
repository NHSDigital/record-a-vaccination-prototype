Any community pharmacy offering NHS vaccinations can sign up.

What it involves

  1. You'll have the option of signing up for a single pharmacy or multiple pharmacies.
  2. Next, we'll ask you to select the pharmacy or pharmacies.
  3. Then you can nominate up to 5 lead administrators, including yourself, who will get access to RAVS at the selected pharmacy or pharmacies.
  4. Your nominated users will get an email with details of how to log in to RAVS.
  5. Once they have logged in, they will be able to add further users at individual pharmacies, including more lead administrators.
  6. Any lead administrator can, in turn, add further users to individual pharmacies.

Signing up for multiple pharmacies

If you have multiple pharmacies you can either:

  • ask each pharmacy to sign up individually
  • sign up for a few pharmacies at the time
  • sign up all your pharmacies at once
  • pale skin

If you have a large chain, you may want to consider signing up for a few pharmacies at the time.

This would allow to you to nominate different lead administrators for each subset of pharmacies, and therefore share the job of adding users to individual pharmacies between more people.

Before you start

If you have any questions about signing up, join a drop-in session with the RAVS team who will be happy to help
