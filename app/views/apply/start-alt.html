You can sign up for either:

  • a single pharmacy
  • multiple pharmacies

How signing up works

  1. Select the pharmacy or pharmacies.
  2. Nominate up to 5 lead administrators, including yourself, who will get access to RAVS at the selected pharmacies.
  3. Once they log in to RAVS, the lead administrators will be able to add more users, including more lead administrators, at individual pharmacies.
  4. Anyone with lead administrator permissions can then add further users to the pharmacy or pharmacies where they have access to RAVS.

Signing for a chain of pharmacies

If you have a large chain, you may want to consider signing up for a few pharmacies at the time.

This would allow you to have different lead administrators for each subset of pharmacies.

Get help and advice

If you have any questions about signing up, join a drop-in session with the RAVS team who will be happy to help
